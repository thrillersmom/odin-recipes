Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Oregano

Directions

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Layer pasta with cheese, meat and tomato
  3. Top with oregano and cheese
  4. Bake for 1 hour on 350 degrees F
  5. Let rest for 10 minutes then cute and serve
