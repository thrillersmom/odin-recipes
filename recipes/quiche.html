Quiche

Description

This is something I eat all the time because it is nutritious and I get to use my farm fresh eggs!

You can switch up the veggies each time, add different protein, add cheese or cream...the possibilities are endless!

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • 1 sweet potato
  • 10-15 brussel sprouts (shredded) or broccoli (if you don't like brussel sprouts)
  • Peppers, mushrooms, asparagus or any other veg you like
  • Bacon, ham, sausage, chicken, any other protein you like
  • Onion
  • Milk, coconut milk, almond, heavy cream
  • Nutritional yeast

Directions

  1. Slice sweet potato in thin rounds and layer on bottom, add shredded brussel sprouts on top of that
  2. Cook bacon, remove and set aside.
  3. Carmelize onions in bacon fat
  4. Mix eggs with milk of choice, nutritional yeast and spices, pour over sweet potatoes
  5. Bake 45 mins at 425F
