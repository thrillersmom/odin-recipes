Cheesecake

Description

This recipe is good

This is a pretty simple and basic recipe

It is a good recipe for a beginner

Ingredients

  • 3 8oz block cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • Pre-baked Cheesecake crust*

Directions

  1. Mix cream cheese and sugar well for several minutes on medium speed in stand mixer with paddle attachment
  2. Add sour cream and vanilla and mix for a few minutes until smooth
  3. Add eggs one at a time mix on low speed, do not overmix!
  4. Pour onto crust and bake for 1 hour at 325F
  5. After 1 hour turn over off and leave cheesecake in over to "cool" for an hour
  6. Cool in fridge overnight then top with whipped cream!
